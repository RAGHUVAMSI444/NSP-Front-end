Scholarship For Girls

  • ‘One Girl’ per family and it can be extended for Two Girl Child per family where the family income is less than Rs. 8 Lakh /annum (In case of married girl child, the income of parents/ in laws whichever is higher is to be considered).
  • The selection of candidate will be made on merit based on qualifying examination to peruse the respective Technical Degree/Diploma course from any of the AICTE approved institution. Amount of scholarship: Tuition Fee of Rs. 30,000/-.
